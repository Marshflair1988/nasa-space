NASA and Space Activity

NASA leads America’s civil space programme: crewed missions, robotic exploration, Earth science, and astrophysics. With international and commercial partners, NASA supports the International Space Station, Artemis missions to the Moon, Mars rovers, and observatories like the James Webb Space Telescope. Understanding the technology and missions behind these efforts helps explain how we reach space and what we learn there.

NASA Image of the Day

NASA’s Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) features a new image or video each day, chosen and explained by professional astronomers. Subjects include galaxies, nebulae, planets, moons, spacecraft, and events like eclipses or meteor showers. The explanation describes what you are seeing and why it is significant.
